Servcies.

We provide a wide variety of services to our customers intended to maximise their profits and growing their market share by making use of a website or web app.

Domain
We will check the availability and register your domain name on your behalf at nominal cost.
Web Hosting
We will host your web site from as little as R 150.00 per month depending on the size of your website or app. We will also host website developed by other companies.
Website Customization
We will customize your website or app at a fixed price determined before we start. It might even be free depending on your needs. You will not get any surprise when you need to make the payment.
Customised email adresses
Depending on the hosting package you take you will receive a number of free customized email addresses. If you need more we can add more. We can negotiate the price depending on your needs.
Resposive Design
All new websites and apps are responsive. This means that it can be used on desktops and hand held devises just the same. No matter how old your website is, we can make it modern and responsive as well.
Secure and Reliable
All website and apps are tested before published. After publishing the customer get 4 weeks to test the site and make sure that there are no faults or problems before making the final payment (if it is not a free web site). If there are any problems we will fix it and extend the 4 week testing period.
